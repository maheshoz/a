Virat Kohli

He is an Indian international cricketer who currently captains the India national team. An elegant right-handed batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world.

He captained India Under-19s to victory at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup in Malaysia, and a few months later, made his ODI debut for India against Sri Lanka at the age of 19. He also plays for the Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League (IPL), and has been the team's captain since 2013.
